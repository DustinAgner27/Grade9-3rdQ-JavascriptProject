CHOOSE LEVEL:

EASY

This is the easy level. No twists or obstacles to affect you. Just a casual and simple level.

MEDIUM

This is the medium level. It has a twist. The bricks are not easy to break. You need to hit the bricks a number of times in order to break them.

HARD

This is the hard level. It has a harder twist. This time, the game area is covered by a colored area so you cant see where the ball will end up.
